Our Product

We have built a moderation engine that can detect millions of illegal websites without needing a human to look at disturbing
content. This engine also helps our highly trained moderators to filter out user generated content which makes them faster
and more accurate than a moderator without the engine.

Moderation Engine

The core of the product is a sophisticated moderation engine. This engine is designed to scan and analyze websites, particularly focusing on identifying and flagging illegal content. It likely uses a combination of algorithms, machine learning, and human oversight to achieve this.

Content Flagging

The primary function of the moderation engine is to flag millions of illegal websites. It can identify content that violates laws, regulations, or community standards. This could include content related to pornography, hate speech, violence, illegal drugs, and more.

Community and Protection

The product places a strong emphasis on protecting communities and children from harmful and disturbing content. This suggests that it not only targets illegal content but also content that may be inappropriate or psychologically harmful, especially for younger users.
